Karnataka Tourisim

One State, Many World.

Karnataka is the third most popular state for tourism and rightly so because of the beautiful forests bustling with wildlife, picturesque hill stations and 507 protected monuments with some of the most famous sculptured temples in India. Karnataka in all has 21 wildlife sanctuaries and 5 National parks.
